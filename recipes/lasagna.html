Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering wide, flat pasta sheets with rich meat sauce (usually made with ground beef or pork), creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella, all baked until bubbling and golden. Hearty and flavorful, it’s a comforting, layered casserole often served as a main course at family gatherings and celebrations.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or pork
  • Onion, garlic
  • Crushed tomatoes, tomato paste
  • Olive oil, salt, pepper, Italian herbs
  • Ricotta, mozzarella, Parmesan
  • Egg (for ricotta mix)

Steps

  1. Cook the meat sauce – Sauté onion and garlic, add ground meat, cook until browned. Add tomatoes, paste, and herbs. Simmer.
  2. Boil lasagna noodles – Cook until al dente (skip if using no-boil noodles).
  3. Mix ricotta layer – Combine ricotta, egg, and some Parmesan.
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Bake
  6. Rest and serve
